IIIT Bangalore is located in Electronics City, Bangalore. It was founded in 1999 and is known for its focus on research and advanced education in Information Technology. The campus is equipped with state-of-the-art labs, a vast library, and collaborative spaces. It has a green, eco-friendly environment. IIIT Bangalore has a diverse student population, and its placement statistics are impressive with graduates working in top companies.
The campus is 9 acres in size.
